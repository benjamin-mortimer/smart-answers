

##Civil partnership in Greece

Contact the [Embassy of Greece](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/foreign-embassies-in-the-uk) or the relevant authorities in Greece (town hall or [local notary public](http://www.notaries-directory.eu/) to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.  

^You should get [legal advice](https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) and check the [travel advice for Greece](https://www.gov.uk/foreign-travel-advice/greece) before making any plans.^

##Naturalisation of your partner if they move to the UK

Your partner can apply to become a British citizen once they’ve lived in the UK for 3 years.
